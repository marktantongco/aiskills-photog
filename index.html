01

Technical Prompt Engineering

Constructing prompts as structured blueprints, not keyword lists.

Scaffold Method

Structured: Subject + Action + Lighting + Lens + Style + Quality

A woman walking + cinematic lighting + 85mm f/1.8 + photorealistic + 4K
Information Priority

Front-load critical elements; AI weights early tokens more heavily

"Oil painting of a castle..." vs "A castle, oil painting..."
Photographic Vocabulary

Use precise technical terms over vague buzzwords

85mm lens, f/2.8, shallow DOF
Camera Movement

Specify complex cinematography in text

pull close-up to wide, dolly zoom, tracking shot
02

Photographic Literacy

Reconstructing real-world physics for professional results.

Lighting Patterns

Rembrandt, Butterfly, Rim, Split, Loop setups

Key 45° high, fill 1/4 power, rim for separation
Lens Selection

85-135mm portrait, 35-50mm standard, 18-24mm wide

85mm for flattering compression + bokeh
Aperture Control

f/1.4-5.6 shallow, f/8-32 deep

f/2.8 isolation / f/11 full sharpness
Advanced Rendering

Global illumination, subsurface scattering

subsurface scattering on skin
03

Strategic Negation & Skin

Telling the AI what NOT to include.

Negative w/ Weights

Exclude unwanted with weighted syntax

(plastic skin:1.4), (extra fingers:1.4)
Skin Realism

Prompt textures + negate synthetic

visible pores | negate: airbrushed, doll
Anatomical Correction

Target common AI errors

(fused fingers, double iris:1.4)
04

Identity & Consistency

Maintaining characters and styles across generations.

Seed Locking

Fix noise pattern for controlled variations

--seed 12345
Reference Tools

--cref, --sref, ControlNet for identity

--cref char.jpg --sref style.jpg
Character Weight

--cw 0 (face) to 100 (full)

--cw 80 preserves face + clothing
Visual-Script Alignment

Score frames vs storyboard

Flag >15% deviation for review
05

Post-Processing & Hybrid

AI generation as the start, not the end.

Iterative Refinement

Keep seed; change one parameter

--seed 12345, weather: rainy → snowy
Inpainting/Outpainting

Surgical edits or expand canvas

Fix hands via inpainting
External AI Enhancement

Topaz, Lightroom, Runway for polish

4K→8K upscale + color grade
Text Overlay

Typography during generation

text: "LAUNCH 2026" bold serif
